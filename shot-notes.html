How it works

Shot·Notes reads what's in your screenshots and reshapes it into something you'd actually want to revise from — not a transcript.

01

Capture

Upload one or several screenshots that belong together — pages of the same chapter, slides from the same lecture.

02

Structure

The content is read, then organised into a title, key points, definitions, and a short self-check quiz.

03

Store

Every set of notes is saved to your library automatically, ready to reopen and read any time.
